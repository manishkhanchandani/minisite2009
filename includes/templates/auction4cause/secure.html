Auctions 4 a Cause is Secure

We use encryption technologies to keep your personal information safe. We also do not show your complete credit card number to you on this site, even though you might have elected to store it with your profile.

We utilize Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption and Verisign, the leader in secure transactions, as our payment processor. This security technology makes it safe to enter and transmit your credit card over the Internet by providing the following protections:

Server authentication — keeps out imposters
Privacy using encryption — keeps out eavesdroppers
Data integrity — keeps out vandals

Furthermore, we ourselves are very protective with our personal information, so you can expect our site and company to be adhering to a strict Privacy Policy.

Please contact us with any security or privacy concerns you might have.
