Explanation of Credits

Profile box details

Free Auction Bid credits:
These are credits you can use in our Free Auctions. You earn 1 free credit when you register to become a member, and you will receive 1 free credit for every paid bid your referred friend places. This means the more your friends and family get involved with this site, the more free credits you get!* Note: Free credits expire in 45 days so make sure to use them in our free auctions.

Bonus Bids:
If you happen to not be the successful bidder in an auction, you will receive a bonus bid credit to use in our special Bonus Auction. The Bonus Auctions are restricted to those who participated in a Main Auction. Bonus credits expire in 45 days.

Refer a Friend:
Invite your friends to see this site. And when they become a member and place a bid in one of the main auctions, you will receive a free bid credit for use in our free auction area. Not just once, but EVERY time your friend places a paid bid*.
