How Do The Auctions Work?

Text Version

 

 

Participating in an auction is quick, easy, safe and secure:

  1. Choose a product to bid on.
  2. Click the  button and enter your desired bid amount.
    • Your bid must be less than or equal to the Get4 price.
      • If your bid is the highest unique bid, you win the auction and get to purchase
        the product for your unique bid amount.
      • It is not possible to buy a product at or below the Get4 price unless you
        win the auction with the highest unique bid.

    • Your selected charity or fundraiser will receive one point for every bid you place.

  3. Choose your method of payment and confirm your bid.

    • You will be charged the specified bid fee for every bid you place.

    • Only the winner of the auction is able to purchase the product.

  4. You will receive a confirmation e-mail verifying your payment, bid amount and product.

  5. When the auction closes, if your bid is the highest unique bid, YOU WIN.

    • An auction can close at any time, but it MUST close once the maximum number of bids for that auction have been received.
    • A unique bid is a bid that is NOT duplicated by another bidder.

    • See the Frequently Asked Questions area for more details on unique bids.

  6. We will notify you via e-mail if you've won and provide a link to purchase the item for your unique bid amount.
  7. Or, if your bid is NOT the highest unique bid, you will receive one Bonus Auction credit for each of your unsuccessful bids, to be used in the Bonus Auction area!

  8. Auctions 4 a Cause donates at least 25% the net auction proceeds to the sponsored charities and up to 65% of net proceeds to the fundraisers.

    • See the Frequently Asked Questions area for more details about the donations.
