Supported Charities

Auctions 4 a Cause is on a mission.  Our company's goal is to make a positive and significant difference throughout our communities.  To accomplish this, Auctions 4 a Cause has developed a new and innovative method to raise money for charities and fundraisers called the Auction 4 Charity. 

Our members are given the chance to purchase great products such as iPods, PS3’s, digital cameras or laptops at 80-90% off retail prices while raising money for the charities we support.  Every quarter Auctions 4 a Cause donates a minimum of 25% of the net proceeds raised through the bidding fees paid to participate in our auctions.  Listed below are the worthy charities and causes we are currently supporting.  As a member you can choose one of these causes for your bid proceeds to benefit.  In addition, you can tell us about your favorite charity and every quarter we will select a user submitted causes to receive a portion of that quarter’s donations.

Auctions 4 a Cause donates to charities every quarter. We want to thank our members for generating, on average, over $10,000 for our charities every quarter! And this quarter is no different with $13,271 donated in July 2008!

Auctions 4 a Cause was one of the first unique bid auction sites on the Internet in 2003 and pioneered the Auction 4 Charity.  Others have tried to imitate our concept, but none have our proven track record and the $10’s of thousands of dollars we have raised for worthy causes.  We are proud to be official partners with some of our charities while others are waiting for us to reach specific donation milestones before forming official partnerships with us.  We wouldn't want it any other way.  Auctions 4 a Cause is committed to proving our worth and we will continually list our donations on this page. 

You can bid with confidence knowing that not only can you win a Nintendo Wii, iPod, digital camera, LCD TV, PlayStation 3, laptop or Xbox 360 for 80-90% off retail prices, but that you are also supporting some great causes in the process.  Thank you for your support and remember to tell your friends about Auctions 4 a Cause.
