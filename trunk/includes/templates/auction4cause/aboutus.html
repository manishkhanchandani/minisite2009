About Us/Customer Service

Thank you for your interest in Auctions4aCause. We strive to offer you the best deals, while donating proceeds to charities.

Auctions 4 a Cause, LLC, a pioneer in the US for "Highest Unique Bid" Auctions, has been holding Unique Bid Auctions since the Fall of 2003. We were the first unique bid auction site to donate any auction proceeds to charity, not only that, but we continue to lead the pack by donating at least 25% of our auction proceeds to the charities we support. By becoming a member, you get to influence how much your selected charity receives by placing bids in our auctions. At Auctions 4 a Cause you can bid with confidence along with over 100,000 members and a proven and reputable company you know has been around for years.

Over the years, Auctions 4 a Cause has donated tens of thousands of dollars to multiple charities (see the Charities page for the current charities you can choose to support) and we continue to donate thousands of dollars every quarter thanks to people like you who participate in our auctions. By bidding, not only do you get the opportunity to get amazing deals, but you also know you are supporting worthy and needy charities.

Auctions4aCause.com features unique bid auctions which are legally classified as skill-based prize competitions. In these blind auctions our members compete for the chance to purchase popular brand new, name brand products at 80-90% off retail prices. The participants bid for a particular item by submitting blind or sealed (i.e. secret) bids: the winner is the bidder who places the highest unique bid. A "unique" bid, in this context, means one in which the amount offered is the only one of that value. Each auction has a defined number of bids that are accepted before it closes and the highest unique bidder gets to purchase the item for their bid amount. For every bid a participant places in an auction, they pay a nominal bidding fee and the charity in the participant's member profile will receive a donation based on their bid(s).

Prior to starting Auctions 4 a Cause, several of our founders were involved with fundraising for charities and had noticed that contributions to charities were suffering. The company was founded after the high-tech industry crash in 2003 in which we saw first hand a donations shortfall and increased need for charitable support. We decided to take matters into our own hands and use years of marketing, engineering and web development experience in pursuit of bettering our society and achieving our dreams.
